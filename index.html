A Confidential & Automated Process

Connecting talent with opportunity in 3 simple steps.

1. Submit Your Profile

Fill out a confidential form detailing your skills, interests, or project needs. Your data is never shown publicly.

2. Automated Matching

Our secure script instantly compares your submission against our private database to find potential skill-based matches.

3. Connect via Email

If a strong match is found, we introduce both parties via a private email. You take it from there!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. This is an official PGIMS platform. Your data is stored in a private database, accessible only to the system administrator for maintenance. The script connects people via email introductions. No lists of students or faculty are ever published on this website.

How does the matching work?

Matching is based on skills and interests. When you submit your profile, you list your qualifications. Our script compares them to the "required skills" listed for projects. If there is an overlap, it triggers a confidential email introduction.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, this platform is 100% free for all students and faculty of Pt. B.D. Sharma PGIMS, Rohtak.
